## October 2024

### Billing & BYOK Launch
**October 29-31, 2024**

<Warning>
  **Major Infrastructure Update:** After rapid growth and credit challenges, we introduced sustainable billing and BYOK support.
</Warning>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Billing Tiers Introduced">
    **October 31, 2024**

    Sustainable pricing to keep Compyle running:

    - **Free:** 500 credits for everyone
    - **Standard:** $10/mo for 2,500 credits
    - **Pro:** $25/mo for 7,500 credits
    - **Pro+:** $50/mo for 17,500 credits

    **Why this happened:**
    - Rapid user growth exceeded startup credits
    - Service outages from Oct 27-29
    - Need sustainable infrastructure
    - Free credits remain available through referrals and social sharing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)">
    **October 29, 2024**

    Use your own Anthropic API key:

    - Create key at console.anthropic.com
    - Connect in Compyle settings
    - Pay Anthropic directly for usage
    - Monitor spend in Anthropic console

    **When to use:**
    - You have existing Anthropic credits
    - Need predictable billing
    - Want direct cost control
    - Temporary solution during credit shortage
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Community Rules Added">
    **October 31, 2024**

    Clear guidelines to keep the community healthy:

    - Respectful collaboration principles
    - Channel organization guidelines
    - Code of conduct for Discord
    - Transparency and building responsibly
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Infrastructure Recovery & Stability
**October 27-30, 2024**

<Accordion title="What Happened: Growth Pains & Recovery">
  **The Challenge:**
  - Traffic spike exceeded infrastructure capacity
  - Redis backlog caused agent unresponsiveness
  - Anthropic rate limits and credit exhaustion
  - Service disruptions Oct 27-29

  **The Response:**
  - Major backend migration for resumable tasks
  - Server limit increases
  - Alternative model deployment (Haiku 4.5)
  - BYOK as stopgap solution
  - Service fully restored Oct 30

  **What We Learned:**
  - Need scalable credit management
  - Importance of multi-model support
  - Value of transparent communication
  - Grateful for patient early users

  **Permanent Migration Note:**
  Tasks created before Oct 26 6PM PST were permanently spun down on Oct 27 6PM PST to complete the resumability migration. Chat history and GitHub code remained accessible.
</Accordion>

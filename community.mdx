### Report Bugs

**If you find a bug**:

1. **Verify it's reproducible** - Can you repeat it?
2. **Check it's not already reported** - Search GitHub issues
3. **Gather details**:
   - What you were doing
   - What happened vs. expected
   - Browser/device info
   - Steps to reproduce
4. **Report via**:
   - [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/compyle) (preferred)
   - Discord #bug-reports
   - Email to [hi@compyle.ai](mailto:hi@compyle.ai)

## Community Guidelines

### Be Respectful

- Respect other members
- Assume good intent
- Provide constructive feedback
- No harassment or discrimination

### Stay On Topic

- Keep conversations relevant
- Use appropriate channels
- Search before asking
- Link to relevant context

### Share Responsibly

- Don't share API keys or credentials
- Protect private information
- No spam or self-promotion
- No solicitation

### Report Issues

If you see violations of community guidelines:
- Report to moderators
- Email [hi@compyle.ai](mailto:hi@compyle.ai)
- DM founders on Discord

## How Roadmap Decisions Are Made

We prioritize based on:

1. **User feedback** - What you vote for
2. **Use cases** - How features enable new workflows
3. **Strategic alignment** - Where Compyle is headed
4. **Technical feasibility** - What's realistic to build
5. **Market demand** - What enterprises need

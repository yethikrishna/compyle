## Vote on Features

Your feedback directly shapes our roadmap. Here's how to influence what we build:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Feedback Board">
    Go to [compyle.ai/feedback](https://compyle.ai/feedback) to see all proposed features
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upvote Your Favorites">
    Click the upvote button on features you want us to prioritize
  </Step>

  <Step title="Suggest New Ideas">
    Don't see your feature? Submit a new request with details
  </Step>

  <Step title="Join Discussions">
    Comment on proposals to share your use case and requirements
  </Step>

  <Step title="Stay Engaged">
    Join [Discord](https://discord.gg/U9djmRTDB4) to discuss directly with our team
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Questions & Feedback

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When will [feature] be available?">
    Check the roadmap sections above for estimated timelines. For specific questions, email [founders@compyle.ai](mailto:founders@compyle.ai) or ask in [Discord](https://discord.gg/U9djmRTDB4).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How accurate are the timelines?">
    Timelines are estimates based on current plans. They may shift based on:
    - User feedback and reprioritization
    - Technical complexity discoveries
    - Resource constraints
    - Market changes

    We update this page quarterly and announce major changes in Discord.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I request a custom feature?">
    Yes! Submit your request at [compyle.ai/feedback](https://compyle.ai/feedback) or email [founders@compyle.ai](mailto:founders@compyle.ai).

    For enterprise customers, we offer design partnerships for custom features.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I report bugs vs. request features?">
    **Bug:** Something doesn't work as expected
    - Report in [Discord](https://discord.gg/U9djmRTDB4) or email [hi@compyle.ai](mailto:hi@compyle.ai)

    **Feature:** New functionality you'd like to see
    - Submit at [compyle.ai/feedback](https://compyle.ai/feedback)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
